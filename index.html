About

Originally from Mena, Arkansas, I’ve lived in Conway since 1998. I’m married to my high school sweetheart, Jamie Steele. We have 2 children, Rachel and Levi.

I graduated from Acorn High School in 1996. In 2000, I graduated from the University of Central Arkansas with a BBA in Information Systems. Since 2005, I’ve worked in Downtown Little Rock for NIC developing eGov solutions for the State of Arkansas.

When I’m not spending time with my family or sitting in front of my computer, I enjoy photographing the world around me with my Nikon.
